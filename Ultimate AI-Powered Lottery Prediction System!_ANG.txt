📝 Lottery Prediction System Description
Our advanced lottery prediction system combines artificial intelligence (AI), statistical modeling, probability analysis, and combinatorial optimization to maximize the chances of winning.

The system processes historical lottery results, analyzes patterns and trends, and generates optimal predicted number sets for the upcoming draw.

🔧 Technologies and Algorithms Used
✅ 📊 Statistical and Probability Analysis:
Hot & Cold Numbers – Identifying the most frequently and least drawn numbers.
Most Common Pairs & Triplets – Finding number combinations that frequently appear together.
Number Distribution & Probability Calculations – Using data-driven insights to predict the most probable outcomes.
✅ 📈 Hybrid AI + Statistical Prediction Models:
1️⃣ LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory – Neural Network)

Analyzes lottery draw sequences to identify hidden patterns.
Predicts numbers with a high probability of repeating in future draws.
2️⃣ ARIMA (AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average)

Time-series model that detects recurring trends in lottery draws.
Especially effective for seasonal patterns and periodic cycles in results.
3️⃣ Holt-Winters (Exponential Smoothing)

Forecasting model that detects long-term increasing trends in number frequency.
✅ 🎡 Wheel System + Smart Number Selection
Wheel System – A combinatorial technique that optimizes number coverage, minimizes redundancy, and maximizes winning potential.
Number Selection Algorithm – Ensures unique number sets, avoiding too many similar combinations.
Best Combination Analysis – Picks numbers with the highest winning potential, based on historical data and frequency analysis.
✅ 📥 Data Processing & System Structure:
📌 fetch_data.py → Fetches and merges historical data into a structured CSV file.
📌 analyze_data.py → Performs statistical analysis, generating reports on number frequency.
📌 predict_numbers.py → Integrates AI models, probability analysis, and combinatorial logic to generate the most optimized predictions.
📌 main.py → The system's core logic → controls data processing, analysis, and prediction generation.
